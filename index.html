Guide

How to Use This Calculator

Four specialized tools for different goals. Pick the tab that matches your objective.

Daily Protein — Best starting point. Enter weight, activity, and goal. The optimal value is your target; anywhere in the range works.
Muscle Building — Uses training experience (0.8–1.2g/lb). Enable LBM if body fat exceeds ~25%.
Weight Loss — Higher protein preserves muscle. Recalculate every 10–15 lbs lost.
Formula

How It Works

Daily Protein (g) = Body Weight (lbs) × Protein Factor (g/lb)

  • Build Muscle: +0.2 to base activity factors
  • Lose Weight: +0.3 to min/optimal, +0.2 to max
  • Deficit: Small 0.9 · Moderate 1.0 · Aggressive 1.1 g/lb

FAQ

Common Questions

How much protein to build muscle?

Aim for 0.7–1.0g per pound. Advanced lifters benefit from 1.1–1.2g/lb.

More protein when cutting?

Yes — 1.0–1.2g/lb preserves muscle during a calorie deficit.

Body weight vs lean mass?

Use LBM above 25% body fat. Otherwise total weight works fine.

30g absorption limit?

A myth. Spread 25–40g across 3–5 meals for optimal synthesis.
